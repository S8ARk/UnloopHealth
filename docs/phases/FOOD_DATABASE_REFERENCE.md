## 🔬 Understand Your Labels

### **Glycemic Index (GI)** - How fast it spikes blood sugar
- **Low GI (0-55)**: Brown rice, oats, dal, beans, vegetables
- **Medium GI (56-69)**: White rice, whole wheat, banana, roti
- **High GI (70+)**: White bread, refined cereal, watermelon

### **Antioxidant Power** - Disease-fighting compounds
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5) = Pomegranate, berries, spinach, broccoli, turmeric
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4) = Tomato, beet, citrus, nuts, seeds
- ⭐⭐⭐ (3) = Carrot, apple, green beans, mushroom
- ⭐⭐ (2) = Chicken, fish, eggs, pasta
- ⭐ (1) = White bread, white rice

### **Omega-3 Content** - Heart and brain health
✅ **High Omega-3**: Salmon, tuna, trout, sardines, walnuts, flax seeds
⚠️ **Moderate**: Eggs, chickpeas
❌ **Low/None**: Grains, dairy, chicken breast
